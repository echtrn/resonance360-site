# Resonance 360 — Site Brief

## Project
Build a clean, professional website for Resonance 360 (R360), a Singapore-based entity founded by Dr Tham Seng Choe, Senior Consultant Radiologist and Clinical Director, subspecialty in MSK and Interventional Radiology.

## Brand
Name: Resonance 360
Tagline: "Precision Radiology. Intelligent Innovation."
Tone: Authoritative and specialist. Clinician-to-clinician for services. Forward-looking and credible for AI partners.
Visual style: Clean, dark navy and white. Subtle tech accent colour. Minimal, professional, modern.

## Target audiences
1. Referring clinicians seeking MSK second opinions or intervention referrals
2. AI companies and vendors seeking a credible clinical partner

## Site structure — 5 sections (single-page scroll or multi-page)

### 1. Home
- Hero with tagline and mission statement
- Two pillars: Clinical Excellence / AI & Innovation
- Two CTAs: "Request a Second Opinion" and "Partner With Us"

### 2. About
- Dr Tham Seng Choe — Senior Consultant Radiologist, Clinical Director, MSK & Interventional subspecialty
- R360 as the vehicle connecting deep clinical expertise with applied AI
- Philosophy: precision, accountability, innovation

### 3. Clinical Services
- MSK Radiology Second Opinions — Expert review of complex or equivocal MSK imaging (spine, joints, soft tissue). For clinicians seeking a specialist read before committing to a management plan.
- MSK & Spine Interventions — Image-guided procedures including joint injections, nerve blocks, and spine interventions. Referral-based.
- Clear referral CTA with contact prompt

### 4. AI & Innovation
- AI Advisory — Bridging healthcare systems with AI companies
- Workflow Redesign — Clinical workflow transformation to harness AI and automation
- Medical Report Generation & Optimisation — AI-assisted structured reporting pipelines
- Thought leadership woven in: embed or display latest LinkedIn posts

### 5. Contact
- Second opinion / referral enquiry form
- Partnership / vendor collaboration enquiry form
- All enquiries go to: echotrain@gmail.com
- Location: Mount Elizabeth Novena, Singapore

## Tech requirements
- Deploy to Vercel
- Mobile responsive
- Fast-loading static site preferred (HTML/CSS/JS or Next.js)
- Contact forms should send to echotrain@gmail.com

## Constraints
- No mention of property or investment activities
- Do not list any other clinical services beyond MSK second opinions and MSK/spine interventions
